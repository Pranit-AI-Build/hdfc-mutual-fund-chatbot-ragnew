## Compliance Rules

✅ **Allowed Topics:**
- NAV (Net Asset Value)
- Expense Ratio
- Exit Load
- Minimum SIP amount
- Fund Size
- Risk Level
- Lock-in period
- Benchmark
- Riskometer

❌ **Not Allowed:**
- Investment recommendations
- Comparisons between funds
- Returns analysis or predictions
- Personal financial advice
- Questions about other AMCs (SBI, ICICI, etc.)
- Non-mutual fund topics (stocks, crypto, FD, etc.)

## Note

All answers are based on factual data from the fund's official sources. The chatbot does not provide investment advice and only answers questions related to HDFC Mutual Fund schemes.
